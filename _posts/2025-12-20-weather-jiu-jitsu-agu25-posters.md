## Overview

I am presenting two Weather Jiu-Jitsu posters at the AGU25 Annual Meeting with Prof. Upmanu Lall's group at Columbia University. The work utilizes the AURORA foundation model and adaptive perturbation to steer weather systems. This is part of my work as an undergraduate student intern with Prof. Upmanu Lall's lab in Columbia University Department of Earth and Environmental Engineering and Arizona State University. 

## Poster Titles

1. **Weather Jiu-Jitsu: Toward Controlling Atmospheric Rivers with Deep Learning Foundation Models and Adaptive Perturbation.**
2. **Weather Jiu-Jitsu: Steering Tropical Cyclones with Small Perturbations of the Jet Stream in Experiments with Deep Learning Foundation Models.**
